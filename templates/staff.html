PCEA Academy-Nairobi: Principal

It is with great joy and gratitude that I welcome you to PCEA Umoja Schools. PCEA Umoja Schools is where we nurture excellence, foster character development, and inspire a lifelong love for learning. Our mission is to provide a holistic education grounded in Christian values, academic rigor, and a commitment to developing responsible global citizens. At PCEA Umoja Academy, we believe every student is uniquely gifted and has the potential to excel in their God-given talents. As we embark on this journey together, I encourage all students to embrace challenges with resilience, parents to remain active partners in their children’s education, and teachers to continue inspiring and empowering the next generation. Together, we will create an environment where dreams take root and flourish. May God bless you all as we work towards a brighter future.

~ Mr. Martin Kaimenyi Mbongori
PCEA Academy-Nairobi: Deputy Principal

Welcoming Message from the Deputy Principal of PCEA Umoja Academy Dear Students, Parents, and Staff, I warmly welcome you all to PCEA Umoja Academy, a place of excellence, growth, and strong Christian values. As we embark on this academic journey together, I encourage each of you to embrace learning with dedication, discipline, and determination. At PCEA Umoja Academy, we are committed to nurturing holistic development—academically, spiritually, and socially. Our dedicated teachers, supportive parents, and hardworking students make this institution a beacon of success. Let us work together in unity, guided by faith and the pursuit of knowledge, to create a bright future for all. May God bless you abundantly in this new academic season.

~ Sharon Alai
